About Us

ZealQK is Tswana full service bakery cafe and catering business based in Kanye. It was founded and is run by Michael Gabaitse Mogamisi out of his love and passion for food. The business began operations in 2018 as an isolated home kitchen and has since become a reputable full time cafe and caterer in town.

Philosophy

Perfection in creating artistic, elegant, memorable dishes with organic ingredients. We create a range of dishes for any dietary requirement from scratch! Most of our sauces are produced in house, bread rolls for burgers and sandwiches are baked in the cafe and our cakes are baked with raw organic products, no premixes.

Quality Assurance

The business has the Kanye District Council Approval and takes due prudence in the daily handling of food; our chefs wear appropriate clothing (hair nets and aprons) whilst on duty. Our premises are given deep and thorough cleaning every week and our food is kept at optimum temperatures and follow safe thawing practices. The business has engaged the services of the local council for refuse collection and there is a fire extinguisher on site.

Product Quality and Pricing

Our approach involves visualisation, planning, tasting and styling of menus to suit any event, be it a large scale hospitality and corporate function or just an intimate private entertainment at home; we will provide a memorable and beautifully presented food, served with care by our friendly and professional staff. We have delicious buffet and a la' carte menus designed for a variety of occassions including weddings, birthdays, confirmations etc. We also have snack platters to suit any cocktail party.
